About Training & Placement Department

Department Overview

The Training and Placement Cell at ANITS functions dynamically as an essential interface linking students with premier industrial enterprises. The unit manages all aspects of industrial training programs, skill gap identification, and end-to-end recruitment pipelines.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To consistently achieve elite placement status by transforming raw human potential into highly proficient technological assets ready to meet engineering challenges.

Mission: To provide thorough, structured interventions in professional styling, software proficiency, industry interaction, and mock evaluation processes to make every student market-ready.

Services Offered

  • Campus Recruitment Drive Management
  • Comprehensive Soft Skills & Aptitude Training
  • In-house Technical Bootcamps & Hackathons
  • Rigorous Mock Technical & HR Interviews
  • Professional Resume Construction Guidance
  • One-on-One Corporate Career Guidance
